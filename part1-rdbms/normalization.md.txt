## Anomaly Analysis

### Insert Anomaly
A new sales representative cannot be added unless an order exists. Since sales representative information is stored only in the orders table, inserting a new sales rep requires creating a dummy order.

### Update Anomaly
Customer information such as customer_city appears in multiple rows if the customer places multiple orders. Updating the city requires modifying every occurrence. If some rows are missed, inconsistent data occurs.

### Delete Anomaly
If an order containing a product is deleted and that product appears only once in the dataset, deleting the order removes the product information entirely from the database.
